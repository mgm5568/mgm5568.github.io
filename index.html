Research Interests

I am a wildlife scientist by training. I am interested in applying principles of population dynamics to address the conservation and management of wildlife, specifically amphibians.

Ph.D. Research

For my PhD, I am using empirical field methods and robust quantitative approaches to learn more about the movement ecology and space use of the Eastern Red-backed Salamander.

Merging Social and Natural Resource Sciences

As I have progressed in my PhD, I am increasingly working within the intersection of social and natural resource sciences to improve science capacity building and more effective management.
